09 / What we don't do

Compliance sounds boring until a number gets banned and every message stops sending. Here's what's permanently off the table, and why.

✕ No personal WhatsApp or iMessage

Automation runs on registered business channels only. Personal accounts get banned running at any real volume, so we never touch them.

✕ No unregistered numbers

Every sending number is A2P 10DLC registered before it goes live. Skip that step and carriers block it outright, silently, no warning. We don't skip it.

✕ No cold texting strangers

SMS only reaches people who already reached out or opted in. If cold outreach is ever part of the plan, it happens over email, never a text blast to a bought list.

✕ No bought lists, no spoofed numbers, no shortcuts that get flagged as spam

If it risks the number's reputation, it's off the table, permanently, not just for now.
